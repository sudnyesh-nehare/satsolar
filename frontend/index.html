About

What is SatSolar?

SatSolar is a solar energy prediction tool built for homeowners, students, and researchers. Select any location, enter the time of day and temperature, and instantly see solar irradiance, energy output, and potential monthly savings — powered by a machine learning model trained on NASA satellite data.

Features

Everything you need to evaluate solar energy

Interactive Map
Click anywhere on the globe to instantly select a location. No address or coordinates needed.
ML Prediction
A Random Forest model trained on 3 years of hourly NASA satellite data delivers accurate irradiance estimates.
Energy Output
Get peak power, daily and monthly energy generation estimates for a standard 5 kW rooftop system.
Bill Savings
See how much you could save monthly on electricity bills based on your location's solar potential.
Smart Advice
Get a plain-language recommendation on whether your location is suitable for solar installation.
Global Coverage
Works for any location on Earth covered by the NASA POWER satellite dataset.

Backed by real satellite data

SatSolar uses the NASA POWER API — a publicly available dataset of hourly solar irradiance and meteorological readings collected from satellites. The prediction model is trained on 3 years of historical data specific to any selected location, giving results grounded in real-world measurements rather than estimates.
